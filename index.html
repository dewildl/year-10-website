Fighter Jets

On 19 April 1944 the first fighter jet was made. It was made in Germany and at Lechfeld Air Base they tested for around two years. Fighter jets are one of the main roles in modern day combat they can be used for multiple roles like air transport, resupplying, air defence and more. Most fighter jet pilots must train for three to four years. There are many different types and ages of fighter jets it starts from gen 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and even 6 generation fighter jets.
